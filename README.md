# Focus Planner

A focused daily planner with drag-and-drop tasks, adjustable work hours, and a built-in Pomodoro timer. No login needed — just enter your email.

## Features

- **Today list** — add tasks and section headers, drag them into the order that fits your day
- **Adjustable work hours** — set your working day (e.g. 9 AM – 5 PM) and see hours remaining
- **Pomodoro timer** — built-in focus timer with configurable work/break durations and a visual progress ring
- **Pomodoro queue** — select specific tasks to include in your focus session; tick them off as you go
- **Email-based sessions** — no password; enter your email to save your planner and access it from any device
- **Recovery link** — lost your session? Enter your email and we'll send a link straight to your planner
